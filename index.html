Our Background


For 25 years, PJSC TEK
"LembergTrans" has been providing
cargo transportation services and is
a leader in the international road
haulage market. There are 300
Euro-6 Volvo trucks in the company
arsenal. Among them are 240 units
with awning semi-trailers of the
"Koegel" and "Schmitz brands" and
60 Schmitz refrigerated semi-trailers.
The state-of-the-art car fleet, rich
experience and high professionalism of
the personnel guarantee the reliable,
timely and safe delivery of your cargo.
